# Code of Conduct

Lumora UI follows the Contributor Covenant standard for open-source participation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about technical problems.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Accept maintainers' decisions when they are made to protect project quality, security, or community safety.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Abuse of issue, discussion, or review channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project. Security or abuse reports should be sent privately using the process in `SECURITY.md`.
